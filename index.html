About me

Hi ! I'm Arnaud. I started my PhD in François Fleuret's Machine Learning group in March 2020. I am trying to forecast wind at high-altitude based on live data. Current forecasts given by National Weather agencies are too sparse in time and space to be reliable to manage air traffic. We are trying to solve that problem by nowcast the wind based on the last aircraft's measurements.

Education
